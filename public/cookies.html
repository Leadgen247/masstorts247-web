1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that websites place on your device to store information. We also use related technologies — such as local storage, session storage, and pixel tags — for similar purposes. In this Policy, "cookies" refers to all of these technologies collectively.

Cookies can be first-party (set by MassTorts247) or third-party (set by services we use, such as Google Analytics or Stripe). They can be session cookies (deleted when you close your browser) or persistent cookies (stored until they expire or you delete them).
